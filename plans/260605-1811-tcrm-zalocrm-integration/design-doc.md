## Problem Statement

Shiseido has 200+ employees managing customer relationships entirely through personal Zalo accounts. There is no centralized system to track:
- Which employee is selling to which customer
- What stage of negotiation each deal is in
- Historical messages and customer preferences
- How to handoff customers when an employee leaves

When employees leave, the company loses customers because there's no way to transfer relationships. TCRM (the existing CRM system) has no Zalo integration, leaving a critical gap in customer data and continuity.

---

## Demand Evidence

**Customer:** Shiseido  
**Scale:** 200+ employees, multiple customer touchpoints  
**Decision maker:** Technology Director  

**Specific pain points observed:**
- Employees organize conversations across 3 separate tools: physical notes, Zalo's document feature, and Notes app
- Common error: forgetting to reply to customer messages (no centralized notification system)
- Customer handoff is manual (messaging back-and-forth), with no audit trail or tracking

**Quote:** "We're losing customers when employees leave because we have no way to know what stage they were at, who they were selling to, or what the customer was promised."

---

## Status Quo

- **TCRM** manages customer records, deals, and pipeline
- **Personal Zalo accounts** handle all messaging with customers
- **No integration:** Zalo conversations exist completely outside TCRM
- **Manual workarounds:** Employees write notes on paper, save to Zalo documents, or use Notes app to track deals
- **Zero handoff mechanism:** When an employee leaves, their customer relationships are lost unless manually transferred via message

---

## Target User & Narrowest Wedge

**Target user:** Shiseido employee (salesperson/support)

**What they need first:**
- QR login to personal Zalo **directly from TCRM** (not in a separate ZaloCRM interface)
- View historical messages **inside TCRM** (no need to jump to another app)
- See customer conversation history organized by contact

**Narrowest wedge (MVP):**
- Employees in TCRM can scan a QR code to authenticate their personal Zalo account
- History of messages with each customer appears inside TCRM
- Read-only view (not editable yet)
- Test with 5 employees + real customers before full rollout

---

## Constraints

- **No module rewrite:** Do not build a Zalo module inside TCRM from scratch. Use ZaloCRM's existing APIs as the integration bridge.
- **User experience:** Everything must happen inside TCRM; employees should not need to open ZaloCRM separately.
- **Timeline:** Technology Director will greenlight immediately if a working demo exists (5 employees can login and see history).

---

## Premises

1. **Real problem, not hypothetical:** Shiseido is actively losing customers due to lack of Zalo integration.
2. **ZaloCRM is the bridge, not a replacement:** TCRM will embed a Zalo module that calls ZaloCRM APIs; no rewriting of ZaloCRM functionality.
3. **If not fixed, revenue impact:** Without integration, Shiseido continues losing customer relationships over the next 3 months.
4. **Fast greenlight:** Technology Director will approve immediately with a working demo (QR login + read-only history).
5. **Employees need notes/reminder/handoff:** Current workarounds (3 apps, paper) signal demand for integrated notes, message reminders, and customer handoff features.

---

## Approaches Considered

### Approach A: Minimal Viable (CHOSEN)
**Embed Zalo login + read-only message history in TCRM**

- QR code scanner integrated into TCRM to authenticate personal Zalo accounts
- Display message history (past 30-90 days) for each customer contact
- Read-only view; no editing or sending yet
- Test with 5 Shiseido employees + real customers
- Uses ZaloCRM API: `POST /api/v1/zalo-accounts/:id/login` (QR flow) + `GET /api/v1/conversations/:id` (history)

**Effort:** S (1-2 weeks)  
**Risk:** Low  
**Pros:**
- Ship fastest; Giám đốc Công nghệ greenlight immediately
- Validate that Shiseido truly wants this (not hypothetical)
- Foundation for future features (notes, reminder, handoff)

**Cons:**
- Does not yet solve customer handoff automation
- Employees still manually manage notes outside TCRM

---

### Approach B: Ideal Architecture (Future)
**Full-featured Zalo integration: login + history + notes + reminder + handoff**

- Everything from Approach A, plus:
- Integrated notes field in TCRM (employees don't need paper/Notes app)
- Message reminder: flag unread customer messages so employees don't forget to reply
- Handoff workflow: when employee leaves, transfer customers to new owner with audit trail

**Effort:** L (3-4 weeks)  
**Risk:** Medium  
**Pros:**
- Solves all pain points (ghi chú, quên reply, chuyển khách)
- Employees use only TCRM, abandon 3-app workaround
- Can be sold to other customers after Shiseido validation

**Cons:**
- Longer development time; uncertainty if Shiseido will greenlight before demo
- More complex (sync notes, scheduler, handoff audit)

---

### Approach C: Standalone Zalo Dashboard (Not Chosen)
**Build custom Zalo dashboard inside TCRM UI (not embedding ZaloCRM interface)**

- TCRM owns the UI/UX completely; calls ZaloCRM backend APIs
- More flexible for future customization
- Avoids embedding another product's interface

**Effort:** M (2-3 weeks)  
**Risk:** Medium  
**Cons:**
- Longer than Approach A; unclear if Giám đốc greenlight before demo
- Duplicates some logic (conversations, login) with ZaloCRM

**Rationale:**
- Shiseido greenlight immediately with working demo → validates demand

## Open Questions

1. **Message retention:** ZaloCRM stores how many months of message history? (30 days? 90 days? Unlimited?)
2. **QR flow:** Does ZaloCRM's QR login session persist on TCRM, or does each refresh require re-auth?
3. **Customer matching:** How do we map Zalo contacts to TCRM customer records? (Zalo UID? Phone? Name match?)
4. **Rate limits:** What are ZaloCRM's API rate limits for `GET /api/v1/conversations/:id`?
